- REGULATORY_GAP: MANDATORY + APPLICABLE + MISSING = violation (must fix)
- SCOPE_GAP: NOT_APPLICABLE or condition not met = OK to be missing

OTHER GAP TYPES:
- Coverage Gap: Topic mentioned but incomplete
