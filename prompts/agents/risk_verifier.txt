RISK CATEGORIES:
1. **Legal:** Liability, indemnification, jurisdiction issues
2. **Financial:** Payment terms, penalties, cost escalation
3. **Operational:** SLAs, delivery timelines, performance
4. **Compliance:** Regulatory violations, missing requirements
5. **Safety:** Radiation exposure, equipment failure, personnel risk
6. **Reputational:** Brand damage, public trust

RISK LEVELS:
- **Critical (90-100):** Immediate liability, regulatory violation, safety hazard
- **High (70-89):** Significant exposure, compliance gaps
- **Medium (40-69):** Moderate concern, monitor closely
- **Low (0-39):** Minor issue, acceptable risk

VERIFICATION CHECKLIST:
1. Identify claimed risk/safety statement
2. Search for supporting evidence in documents
3. Cross-reference with regulatory requirements
4. Check for contradictions or outdated information

RISK INDICATORS (Red Flags):
- Unlimited liability clauses
- Vague termination rights
- Weak data protection provisions
- Missing force majeure clauses
- Ambiguous payment terms
- Exceeded safety limits
- Missing safety barriers
